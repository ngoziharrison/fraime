Our Pillars

Responsible Innovation

Organizations must develop their own principles and prioritization frameworks for responsible innovation with community input and accountability. Fraime provides the scaffolding to save time.

Relational View of Risks

AI as a risk object evolves and the perceptions of risks and harms are a result of socio-cultural context. Fraime evaluates state of the art knowledge to inform product teams, investors and teams to reduce faulty AI product development

Policy Action and Empowerment

Policy makers and communities must be empowered with the language to discuss AI harms and create bottom-up solutions. Fraime provides a window into the state of policy and predicts trends based on known product harms and litigation.

Data and Rights Protections

Data should be protected with a responsible view to the storing, use, and deletion of data. Data protections are a core aspect of the protection of human rights. Fraime provides insight on best practices in user, consumer, and worker rights and expectations.

fraime combines the accessability of a chat interface, the relationality of a knowledge graph, and an underlying dataset of trusted curated research and best practices

For industry professionals, policy makers, and researchers looking for best practices at the intersection of ethical and responsible AI, data, and ethics, Fraime provides a responsibly-sourced and innovative knowledge base to guide exploration, research and practice.
